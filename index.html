About

Student home “De Looyackers” is an apartment complex for people studying in the Eindhoven area, the Netherlands. In total it has 82 apartments. It is located not far from the city centre at the perimeter of the Stadswandelpark (that's why many of our activities are in the park). For information about renting an apartment, visit the website of the student housing agency, Vestide.

The flat has an active resident council. We regularly organize activities, like drinks, dinners or a movie nights. We also throw a big party every year. Check back this page or follow a social feed to keep informed about upcoming activities. Interested in joining the resident council? Send us a message or drop by when we have drinks.
